What is the practical purpose of the $KPOP token?

The $KPOP token is meant to fulfill two primary functions: redistribution rewards and to serve as a digital currency. Holders of the $KPOP token will be entitled to redistribution awards. Users, including those unfamiliar with crypto, will be allowed to use the token as a payment solution for day-to-day services and online products such as freelancing, affiliate payouts, etc. The coin will also be added to leading crypto billers, compelling many websites to accept it as a currency. Also we are building our own crypto payment gateway. To ensure high adoptability of the $KPOP token, our wallet app is intentionally designed to be user-friendly and fast, making it suitable for everyone.

What makes KPOP unique?

$KPOP was conceived as a payment platform that utilizes cryptocurrency as its main means of conducting transactions while providing many benefits and incentives for all of its users. As well as serving as the currency on the gateway platform, $KPOP gives its holders a small percentage in token reflections for each transaction paid by customers in online stores.

What is buyback?

A buyback, simply explained, is carried out by companies to either reduce the total supply of its shares on the market, or to prevent other shareholders from getting the biggest slice of the company control pie. KPOP Team has set this feature up to help reduce the circulation of the $KPOP token, in order to add more value to the limited ones available, and also to keep whales at bay.

How does KPOP auto buyback work?

$KPOP automatically charges a 1% buyback tax on each transaction - these are stored inside the contract. Whenever a transaction occurs, our auto-buy-back feature uses a fraction of the buyback amount to automatically purchase tokens from the liquidity pool. Those tokens are immediately burned after purchase.

Why is Auto buyback Awesome?

After the $KPOP gets bought from the liquidity pool, the new BNB amount is added to the pool and the amount of the tokens in circulation are reduced, thereby leading to an increase in $KPOP price.

How to buy $KPOP?

First you'll need a BEP20 supported wallet, such as Metamask. You will need to buy some BNB. When you have these ready, copy our contact address and head over to PancakeSwap to swap BNB for Kpop Token. Remember that each transaction on Binance Smart Chain costs BNB (this is called the "gas fee" and it's paid to miners to keep the network running).

How does PancakeSwap work?

It is pretty simple: It is an exchange that allows you to trade your favourite token on the DEFI network. It is the biggest exchange platform on the DEFI network, and can be accessed through the DAPPS of your TrustWallet app, or through the built-in browser of your Metamask wallet.

My swap failed with code "INSUFFICIENT_OUTPUT_AMOUNT", what happened?

If you are trying to buy $KPOP but you keep getting the error message: "INSUCCIFIENT_OUTPUT_AMOUNT", do not panic. Bear in mind that Slippage tolerance needs to be set to a minimum of 2% when you buy, and 2% when you sell.

How to buy on PinkSale?

Open the presale link and connect Metamask, if you use Chrome on PC/Mac, or use Metamask built-in browser on mobile. Enter amount of BNB you want to contribute. Press CONTRIBUTE button then Confirm Metamask prompt that will be shown to you. You can claim your token after the presale has ended, by using the same presale link, clicking on "claim token" and approving the Metamask prompt.
